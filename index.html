About us

Mood Indigo or MoodI, as it is fondly called, is the annual cultural festival of IIT Bombay, and the largest in Asia. Held towards the end of December every year, it attracts a whopping crowd of over 1,39,000 students from more than 1700 colleges nationwide. Mood Indigo is devoted to creating an unforgettable experience for all its visitors, simultaneously providing unique opportunities to the youth for showcasing their talents in front of extremely large audiences and top-notch judges. Being a massive platform for providing cultural exchange, Mood Indigo 2017 is ready to take all its visitors on a delightful ride through its theme ‘La Fete Carnaval’. The 47th edition of the festival will be held from the 22nd December to 25th December, 2017. Join us for a glorious carnival!
